## Week 1 — The Story

### Post 1: "Why I built this" (personal, founder story)
- Started in 2023 wanting to automate healthcare via WhatsApp
- Ended up building the platform instead of the product (the classic side-project trap)
- Now it's open source and running in production
- Link to repo

### Post 2: "The cost problem" (problem-oriented, provocative)
- Every WhatsApp BSP charges per-message markups on top of Meta's fees
- Self-hosting eliminates the middleman — you pay Meta directly
- Quick comparison: SaaS BSP ($25-300/mo + per-message) vs OpenBSP ($0 + Supabase hosting)
