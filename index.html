OpenBSP

Open-source WhatsApp Business Platform

OpenBSP is a self-hostable, multi-tenant platform that connects to Meta's WhatsApp Business API. AI agents are first-class — connect any LLM with tools (SQL, HTTP, MCP, human handoff). Run it yourself or offer it to clients as a Meta Business Partner.
